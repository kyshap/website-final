Providence is a thriving city full of young college students. The nightlife scene is popping because of the local college. Providence attracts so many young people because it has a relaxed vibe during the day and a large clubbing scene at night. The city is broken up into sections; the East Side, the West Side, the South Side, the North Side, and the downcity or downtown area. Almost all of Providence's nightlife happens in the downtown area.
